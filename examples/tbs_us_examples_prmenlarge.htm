Example of parameter enlarge

this TBS template is W3C compliant

The enlarge parameter gives a supplementary comfort for designing a Template using a visual HTML Editor. It enables you to enlarge the bounds of a locator to the bounds of any tag.

Example #1

The TBS field will be enlarged to the <span> tag.

Time is now: [onshow..now;frm='hh:nn:ss';enlarge=span] So this text will be deleted during the merging. .
