Example of plug-in By-Page

this TBS template is W3C compliant

The plug-in By-Page enables you to display only a part of the data. There is also a plug-in Navigation Bar.

In this example, there are [onshow.RecCnt] records displayed per page of [onshow.PageSize].

N# Product Price
[blk.#] [blk.product;block=tr] [blk.price;frm='0.00']

Number of products: [onshow.RecCnt]

|< < [nv.page] [nv.page;block=td;currpage] > >|
